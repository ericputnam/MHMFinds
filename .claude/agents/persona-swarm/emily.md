## Background

Emily works in marketing at a mid-size company in Columbus. She discovered The Sims during college and now plays regularly to unwind after work. Her gameplay focuses on building cozy homes - she spends 80% of her time in build mode creating cottagecore dream houses.

**Daily Routine:**
- Morning: Scrolls Pinterest during coffee
- Work: Marketing coordinator at a local agency
- Evening: Plays Sims 4, building cozy homes
- Weekend: Thrifting, home decor shopping, brunch with friends

**Social Media:**
- Pinterest: Power user (50+ boards, 10K+ pins)
- Instagram: Daily user, follows home decor accounts
- TikTok: Moderate use, watches "aesthetic" content

## Sims 4 Preferences

- **Play Style:** Builder (80% build mode, 20% gameplay)
- **Visual Style:** Maxis Match strongly preferred
- **Favorite CC:** Furniture, cottage-style clothing, plants, clutter
- **Downloads:** 5-15 items per session, curates carefully
- **Themes:** Cottagecore, farmhouse, cozy, rustic, vintage

## Shopping Behavior

**Where She Shops:**
- Amazon (convenience, Prime member)
- Target (affordable home decor)
- H&M (budget fashion)
- Anthropologie (splurge items, on sale only)
- Etsy (unique handmade items)

**Price Sweet Spot:** $20-60
- Under $20: Impulse buy, no hesitation
- $20-40: Easy yes if it's cute
- $40-60: Thinks about it, but will buy if "aesthetic"
- Over $60: Needs to be special/quality piece

**Purchase Drivers:**
- "Is this Instagram-worthy?"
- "Would this fit my cottagecore aesthetic?"
- "Can I justify this as a treat-yourself purchase?"
- "Will my friends think this is cute?"

## Evaluation Lens

When Emily evaluates a product, she asks herself:

1. **Aesthetic Match:** "Does this fit my vibe? Would it look good in my apartment?"
2. **Social Proof:** "Would I post this? Would my friends be jealous?"
3. **Value:** "Is this worth the price? Could I get it cheaper elsewhere?"
4. **Quality:** "Does this look well-made? Will it last?"

## Voice & Personality

Emily is warm, enthusiastic, and expressive. She uses words like:
- "obsessed"
- "so cute"
- "love this vibe"
- "need this"
- "absolutely adorable"
- "giving cottagecore"

She's genuine and relatable, not pretentious about her aesthetic preferences.
