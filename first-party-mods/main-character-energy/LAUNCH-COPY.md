## Patreon post

**Title:** We made a trait pack. One of the traits is Delulu.

**Body:**

We spend all day cataloguing other people's CC, and at some point you start
keeping a list of things nobody has made yet. This pack is four entries off
that list.

Main Character Energy is a set of four CAS personality traits:

- Main Character — permanent +1 Confident. The plot bends around them and they know it.
- Golden Retriever Energy — permanent +1 Playful. No thoughts, just enthusiasm.
- Delulu — permanent +1 Happy. The delulu is, in fact, the solulu.
- Cottagecore Dreamer — permanent +1 Inspired. Mentally, they are baking bread in a meadow. Physically, they're at work.

The buffs are weight 1 on purpose. A trait that locks your Sim into one
emotion forever gets old by the second play session — these nudge the
baseline instead, so your Delulu Sim still gets sad when the stove catches
fire. They just recover suspiciously fast.

The boring details that matter: base game only, no packs, Teen through Elder.
It's a pure tuning mod, so you don't even need Script Mods turned on — just
"Enable Custom Content and Mods" in Game Options. The file is 32 kilobytes.

Download: [LINK]

Drop the .package straight into your Mods folder, restart, and check the
trait picker in CAS. If a trait shows up with blank text or a moodlet doesn't
stick, say so in the comments and we'll ship a fix — this is our first
first-party mod and we want it solid.

And if you make a Sim with one of these, we want to see them.
